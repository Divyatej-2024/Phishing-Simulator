Level 17: Smishing (SMS Phishing)

You get a text message on your phone from an unknown number. It reads: "Royal Mail: Your parcel has been held at our depot. A £1.99 shipping fee is required for redelivery. Please visit: track-my-item.net to pay."

SMS Message: "Royal Mail: Your parcel has a pending shipping fee. Visit track-my-item.net to resolve this."
