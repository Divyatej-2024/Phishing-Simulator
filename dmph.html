Level 18: Domain Phishing (Typosquatting)

You get a "Security Alert" email that seems to be from Microsoft. It says there was an unusual sign-in to your account. The sender's email address is "security@microsft.com". It urges you to click a link to verify your recent activity.

From: security@microsft.com
"We detected an unusual sign-in attempt. If this wasn't you, please secure your account here."
