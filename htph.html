Level 6: HTTPS Phishing

You get an email about a security update for your cloud storage account. You click the link and your browser shows a padlock icon and "https://" in the address bar, indicating a secure connection. The URL is "https://www.dropbox-security-update.com". The page asks you to log in to apply the update.

Website Prompt: "Your account is not protected by our latest security patch. Please sign in to continue."
