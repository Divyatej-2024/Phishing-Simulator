Level 8: Angler Phishing

Your flight was delayed, so you post a complaint on social media, tagging the airline. Moments later, you get a reply from another account with a similar name and logo. They apologize and ask you to send them a direct message with your booking reference and passport number to "expedite your compensation claim."

Fake Support (@AirlineHelp_UK): "We are so sorry for the delay. Please DM us your full name, booking reference, and passport number so we can assist you with your claim immediately."
