Level 10: Vishing (Voice Phishing)

You receive an automated phone call. A recorded voice claims to be from HMRC and states that a lawsuit has been filed against you for tax evasion. It warns that local police will arrest you within the hour unless you press 1 to speak with an agent immediately to settle your case.

Robocall: "[BEEP] This is an urgent message from HM Revenue and Customs. An arrest warrant has been issued under your name. To avoid legal consequences, press 1 now."
