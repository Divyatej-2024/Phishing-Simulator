Level 12: Pop-up Phishing

While browsing a news article, a large, flashing red pop-up window takes over your screen. It looks like a system alert and loudly proclaims: "WARNING! Your computer is infected with 5 viruses! Immediate action required!" It has a button to "Scan and Remove Viruses Now".

Pop-up Alert: "Your system is at risk. We have detected spyware and malware. Click here for a free scan to protect your data."
