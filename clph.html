Level 9: Clone Phishing

You receive an email that looks identical to a shipping confirmation you got yesterday from a major online retailer. This new email says there's been an "update" to your order. It has all the same graphics and text, but it asks you to click a new link to view the changes.

From: Online Retailer
"There has been an important update to your recent order #845-1934710. Please click here to view the updated invoice and delivery details."
