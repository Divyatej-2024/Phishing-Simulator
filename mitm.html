Level 19: Man-in-the-Middle Attacks

You're using the free Wi-Fi at a local cafe to do some online banking. You type in your bank's address, the page loads, and it has the correct URL and even a security padlock. However, your browser gives you a brief warning that the "site's security certificate is not trusted," but gives you an option to proceed anyway.

Browser Warning: "Your connection is not private. Attackers might be trying to steal your information... [Proceed to site (unsafe)]"
